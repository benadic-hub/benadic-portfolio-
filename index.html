Story Overview

From a college entrepreneur with big dreams to a global innovation pioneer leading technological transformation

2015

Early Entrepreneurship

While still in college, I launched my first venture 'Offer Zone 360' - an e-commerce platform selling branded surplus men's apparel at discounted prices. This wasn't just a business; it was my introduction to the real world of commerce, teaching me invaluable lessons about market dynamics, customer psychology, and the critical importance of timing in business.

2019-2023

Strategic Skill Building

After completing my Bachelor's in Electronics and Communication Engineering from Jeppiaar Engineering College, I embarked on a carefully planned freelance career. This wasn't random job-hopping - it was strategic skill acquisition. I deliberately progressed from UI/UX Designer to Full-Stack Developer, then to Digital Marketing Specialist, and finally to Business Analyst. Each role built upon the previous, creating a comprehensive foundation for digital entrepreneurship.

Discovery

Global Research & Discovery

The turning point came during my solo international research expeditions. I traveled across 9 countries - Dubai, Singapore, Malaysia, Thailand, Sri Lanka, Sharjah, Bahrain, and made three intensive visits to China's Canton Fair. What I discovered changed everything. I witnessed firsthand a staggering 30-50 year technology gap between China and other developing nations, including my home country. This wasn't just an observation - it was a revelation that would define my life's mission.

Strategy

The Tech-First Strategy

I realized that mastering technology first would enable me to implement cutting-edge insights across traditional industries like hydroponics, cosmetics, FMCG products, and cool drinks. Technology isn't just my business - it's my gateway to revolutionizing every sector I touch.

Present

Current Mission

Today, I lead Cossmicrings Solutions Private Limited with a team of 15 professionals, focusing on AI, blockchain, IoT, and NFC technologies. We're not just building products - we're architecting the future, with our first NFC product launching in just 100 days.
